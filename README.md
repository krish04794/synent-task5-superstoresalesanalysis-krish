# Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## Problem Statement

Businesses generate large amounts of sales data that can be used to evaluate performance and support decision-making. The objective of this project is to analyze Superstore sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and profitability across different product categories.

---

## Dataset Details

**Dataset Name:** Sample Superstore Dataset

**Source:** Kaggle - Superstore Sales Dataset

**Number of Records:** 9,994

**Number of Features:** 21

### Key Features

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Ship Date
* Customer Name
* Segment
* Country
* City
* State
* Region
* Product Name
* Category
* Sub-Category
* Sales
* Quantity
* Discount
* Profit

### Business Metrics Analyzed

* Revenue (Sales)
* Profit
* Product Performance
* Monthly Trends

---

## Approach

The following analysis steps were performed:

### 1. Data Loading

* Imported the dataset using Pandas.
* Examined dataset dimensions and sample records.

### 2. Data Understanding

* Checked data types and dataset structure.
* Identified missing values.
* Generated summary statistics.

### 3. Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis

* Converted Order Date into datetime format.
* Extracted monthly sales information.
* Calculated total monthly revenue.
* Visualized revenue trends using line charts.

### 4. Top-Selling Product Analysis

* Grouped data by Product Name.
* Calculated total sales for each product.
* Identified the top 10 products based on revenue.
* Visualized results using bar charts.

### 5. Profit Analysis

* Grouped data by Category.
* Calculated total profit generated by each category.
* Compared category-wise profitability.
* Determined the most profitable business category.

### 6. Business Insights Generation

* Calculated overall sales and profit.
* Identified high-performing products and categories.
* Evaluated business growth patterns.

---

## Results

### Key Findings

#### Monthly Revenue Trends

* Revenue fluctuated across different months.
* Certain periods generated significantly higher sales, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

#### Top-Selling Products

* A small group of products contributed a large portion of total revenue.
* These products can be prioritized for inventory management and marketing campaigns.

#### Profitability Analysis

* Profit varied across product categories.
* Some categories generated higher profits despite lower sales volumes.
* The most profitable category was identified through category-wise profit comparison.

#### Overall Business Performance

* Total Sales and Total Profit were calculated.
* Revenue and profitability metrics provided valuable insights into business performance.
